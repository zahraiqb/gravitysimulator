# Gravitational Model
This simulation uses Newtonian gravity. 
## which describes gravity as a resultant force acting between all objects with a mass

$$
F = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}
$$

where:
- $F$ is the gravitational force
- $G$ is the gravitational constant (6.69 x10^-11) 
- $m_1, m_2$ are masses of the interacting objects
- $r$ is the distance between bodies
